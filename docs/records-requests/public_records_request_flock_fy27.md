### Request

Pursuant to Iowa Code Chapter 22, I request the following public records:

#### 1. Itemized breakdown of Police Department Account 522101 (Computer Software) for FY27

The FY27 Adopted Budget Book — Supplemental Information shows the Police Department's **522101 Computer Software** line item at **$488,239** (page 74), an increase of **$120,579 (+33%)** from FY26's adopted amount of $367,660.

I request a detailed breakdown showing:
- Each individual software subscription, license, or service included in this total
- Vendor name for each item
- Annual cost for each item
- Contract term (start date, end date, renewal terms)
- Whether the expenditure is operating or capital

#### 2. Flock Safety / ALPR Program Documentation

Specifically, I request documentation identifying:
- Whether the Flock Safety Automated License Plate Reader (ALPR) program is included in the FY27 Police Department 522101 Computer Software line item
- If yes: the exact dollar amount budgeted for Flock Safety in FY27
- If no: the account number and line item where Flock Safety is budgeted in FY27
- The current status of the Flock Safety contract (active, expired, renewed, renegotiated, or terminated)
- Any appropriations, transfers, or encumbrances related to Flock Safety for FY27

#### 3. FY26 vs FY27 Comparison Documentation

The FY26 budget book shows Police 522101 Computer Software increased by $245,410 — a change widely understood to reflect the $225,000/year Flock Safety contract approved June 25, 2024. The FY27 book provides no narrative explanation for the additional $120,579 increase. I request:
- Internal budget memos, emails, or communications explaining the FY27 Police Computer Software increase
- Council agenda items, packets, or presentations referencing this increase
- Department head budget request forms for FY27 Police discretionary expenses

#### 4. Council Packet Attachments

The June 25, 2024 City Council agenda included a Flock Safety budgetary quote ("Cedar Rapids IA - LE Proposal 2024.pdf") as a consent agenda attachment. I request:
- A copy of this document, if not already publicly available
- Any subsequent amendments, renewals, or contract modifications to the original Flock Safety agreement

### Background / Context

The City of Cedar Rapids operates a network of **70 Flock Safety Falcon LPR cameras and 5 Condor PTZ cameras** pursuant to a 24-month contract with an annual recurring fee of **$225,000** and total contract value of **$499,250**.

The original contract was approved June 25, 2024, with a 24-month term ending **June 24, 2026** — approximately **48 days from the date of this request**.

Neither the FY26 nor FY27 Adopted Budget Books explicitly name "Flock Safety," "ALPR," "license plate reader," or related terms in any Police Department line item. The FY26 budget shows a mathematically obvious $225,000 increase in Computer Software coinciding with the Flock contract start. The FY27 budget provides **zero transparency** regarding:
- Whether Flock is renewed, renegotiated, or ending
- Whether the $225,000 annual fee is included in the $488,239 total
- What accounts for the additional $120,579 increase

This request seeks basic fiscal transparency about a **half-million-dollar surveillance program** affecting the civil liberties of all Cedar Rapids residents.
